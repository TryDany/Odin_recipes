Pasta

Ingredients

  1. 1 (16 ounce) package fusilli (spiral) pasta
  2. 3 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  3. ½ pound provolone cheese, cubed
  4. ½ pound salami, cubed
  5. ¼ pound sliced pepperoni, cut in half
  6. 1 large green bell pepper, cut into 1 inch pieces
  7. 1 (10 ounce) can black olives, drained
